Reel 2022

Rituals

Fourteen designers. 63 projects. What a year to be in the room.

Team of 14 designers across UX, UI, and motion. As lead UX designer I shaped flows, set direction, and had a meaningful say across many of the 63 projects, not always end-responsible, but always in the mix.

A year's worth of UI, motion, and experience design captured in two minutes

Key Takeaways

Sixty-three projects in a year sounds like a lot, because it is. But when you're surrounded by talented motion designers, UI designers, and a team that cares about design, it stops feeling like a volume problem and starts feeling like a privilege.

My role wasn't to own the pixels. It was to make sure every project had a clear UX direction, that the interactions made sense, the flows were coherent, and the experience felt like Rituals. Sometimes that meant facilitating. Sometimes it meant pushing back.

The reel is mostly UI and motion, beautiful work by a talented team. What you don't see is the thinking underneath it. That's where I lived.
